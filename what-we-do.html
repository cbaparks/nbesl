NB Environmental Services Ltd (NBESL) became incorporated in 2004. Previously, the organization was operated as a sole proprietorship known as Trinidad and Tobago Recycling Centre (TTRC) which began operations in 2000. TTRC was founded by Neil Benjamin with the purpose of protecting the environment by the provision of premier Environmental Transport & Disposal services. With one truck crew but no truck, TTRC rented vacuum trucks in an effort to meet the needs of its increasing clientele.

While the organization's name and structure has changed since 2000, the excellent service by which we were identified has continued and improved. Our team is led by our intelligent and dynamic Managing Director, Mr Benjamin, and has grown to include thirty (30) strong willed, competent employees now equipped with a fleet of vehicles which includes six [6] vacuum trucks, three [3] flatbed trucks with attached cranes, one [1] dumper truck, one [1] tractor truck with a 40 ft trailer, one [1] hook loader and a ton and a half truck. There is also five [5] other vehicles used for transporting of employees to and from work as well as making deliveries. These are all stored at the company’s warehouse located in Freeport. The range of services has been extended to include a variety of other services. (List attached)

NB Environmental Services Ltd was founded with the purpose of protecting the environment and our team will continue to utilize their vast experience and wealth of knowledge to meet and surpass any and all customer needs in this area. The ever evolving technologies of this century have ensured NBESL’s exploration of new growth opportunities in this field based on our customers’ needs.

Liquid Waste Disposal

Service:

  • Stainless Steel Vacuum Tankers with licensed drivers, truck attendants and a supervisor are provided to efficiently and reliably collect and dispose these industrial hazardous and non-hazardous wastes - engine oil, bilge water, waste water, sludge oil and all hydro-carbon liquids - to an approved disposal site, at all times adhering to the safety standards set out by the Environmental Management Authority (E.M.A.). An escort transporting emergency response equipment always accompanies the tanker. As operations are completed, tankers are taken to a bioremediation facility for cleaning and de-contamination. Temporary Waste staging is also a service provided for liquid waste transportation and disposal.
